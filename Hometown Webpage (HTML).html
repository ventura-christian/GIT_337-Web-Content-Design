Whittier, CA | Zip Code: 90604

Location

Whittier California is situated in southeastern Los Angeles County, approximately 12 miles southeast of downtown Los Angeles, and is part of the Gateway Cities region, providing a central location between L.A. and Orange County. The 90604 ZIP code encompasses areas such as East Whittier, South Whittier, and East La Mirada, covering about 4.2 square miles. The area is characterized by a predominantly Hispanic community, a median household income around $88,000, and a dense suburban landscape.

About

Historic Roots and Founding

Whittier was founded in the late 19th century by Quakers and named after the poet John Greenleaf Whittier, who was an advocate for abolition and education. The city's historic Uptown district still reflects its Quaker heritage through preserved architecture and community-centered values.

Education and Local Institutions

Whittier is home to Whittier College, a private liberal arts institution known for its intimate learning environment and strong community involvement. The city also supports a range of public and private schools, making education a central focus for residents.

Parks and Outdoor Recreation

With over 400 acres of parkland, including the scenic Whittier Greenway Trail and Hellman Park, the city offers extensive outdoor recreation for residents and visitors. Hiking, biking, and community events are common, promoting an active lifestyle within a suburban environment.

Things To Do

  • Explore Pío Pico State Historic Park: Visit the restored adobe home of California's last Mexican governor, Pío Pico, and learn about the state's rich history in this serene park setting.
  • Hike the Whittier Hills for Panoramic Views: Embark on scenic trails in the Whittier Hills, offering breathtaking vistas of downtown Los Angeles, the Pacific Ocean, and even Catalina Island.
  • Experience the Whittier Farmers Market: Held every Friday in Uptown Whittier, this vibrant market offers fresh produce, artisanal goods, and live entertainment, providing a lively community atmosphere.
